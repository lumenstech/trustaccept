## Outreach Message

Quick question — are you currently documenting who approves high-risk AI-agent actions, vulnerability exceptions, or identity/access exceptions before they happen?

We’re launching TrustAccept: a cyber risk acceptance desk that turns risky actions and findings into clean accept/reject/remediate records with owner, reason, compensating controls, expiration date, and evidence PDF.

Useful when a customer, auditor, CISO, or CTO asks: “Who accepted this risk and why?”

## Avoid Legal/Compliance Overclaiming

Never say:
- We certify you
- We make you compliant
- CISA-approved
- NIST-certified
- Guaranteed safe
- Risk eliminated

Say:
- Designed to support
- NIST-aligned
- CISA KEV-aware
- Evidence-ready
- Framework-informed
- Helps document
- Helps route decisions
- Helps maintain an approval trail

## MVP Fulfillment Workflow

1. Customer submits risk/finding/action.
2. Intake call if needed.
3. Analyst reviews context.
4. Create decision register entry.
5. Draft risk acceptance memo.
6. Add compensating controls and expiration date.
7. Send PDF evidence packet.
8. Offer monthly Evidence Desk.
