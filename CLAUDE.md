# TrustAccept Project Memory

## Product Thesis

TrustAccept is not a generic approval app. TrustAccept is the cyber risk decision and evidence layer for high-risk AI, identity, vulnerability, secure release, and device-access events.

## Core Positioning

TrustAccept helps teams accept, reject, escalate, and document high-risk cyber and AI actions before they become incidents, audit gaps, or customer security blockers.

## One-Line Positioning

Cyber risk acceptance and approval infrastructure for AI agents, identity events, vulnerability exceptions, secure software releases, and device access.

## Brand Architecture

- Lumens Technology = parent company / implementation and integration services.
- SequenceNow = identity workflow, MFA, WhatsApp/SMS/email approval delivery, Auth0/Okta/Auth0 Marketplace-facing integration rail.
- TrustAccept = cyber risk decision, approval, risk acceptance, evidence, and audit trail layer.

## Important Constraint

Do not position TrustAccept as:
- A trust badge
- A business verification product
- A review platform
- A fraud-only product
- An invoice product
- A trades marketplace
- A full GRC replacement
- A compliance automation clone
- A scanner
- A pen-test marketplace
- An MDR/SOC replacement

## Better Category

TrustAccept should define a narrower category:

Cyber Risk Acceptance Desk

or

Human Approval Layer for High-Risk Cyber and AI Actions

## Strategic Opinion

TrustAccept should not start as a pure self-serve SaaS with $49/month pricing. The fastest path to monetization is a hybrid of:
1. Productized risk acceptance services.
2. Light workflow/API demo.
3. Manual fulfillment behind the scenes.
4. Recurring managed evidence desk.
